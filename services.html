Our Services

Design your UI/UX

Your site will be beautiful. We focus on the overall user experience and identify structural opportunities for increased conversion. During this stage, we provide wireframes and architecture to inform your overall website structure. From there, our designers create modern interfaces, illustrations, imagery and animations to give your store a unique look and feel.

Development

Our certified in-house developers leverage BigCommerce’s robust functionality to bring your store to life. Using custom responsive website development, we guide you through our technical and quality assurance process to make sure your store performs the way it should. We also create custom integrations with third-party tools to help you leverage the full capabilities of your existing technology stack.

Branding and Brand Story

Have you ever noticed that most brands websites are all about them! That’s great for their ego but it doesn’t sell anything. We can help you organize your ‘Brand Story’ into a very easy to convey blueprint that motivates your customer to act. This can be used as the framework for all of your marketing communication.

Platform to Platform Migration

Shopify is clunky and limiting. Magento comes with way too much cost and too many headaches. If you're on another platform, book a call with us and we’ll show you why you’ll wish you switched to BigCommerce years ago!

Strategy and Analytics

As seasoned marketers we can provide color to your marketing strategy and provide feedback to help you put your spend in the right places.
